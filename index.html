About

Welcome to the official website of Harishankar Kopperi, an ambitious Ph.D. student currently pursuing studies at the Academy of Scientific and Innovative Research (AcSIR), within the esteemed CSIR-Indian Institute of Chemical Technology (CSIR-IICT). Harishankar's research endeavors focus on several key areas within the realm of sustainable processes, with a primary emphasis on leveraging Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) approaches for the design of environmentally responsible products and processes. His commitment extends to crafting sustainable roadmaps geared towards achieving Net-zero targets, and he is actively involved in formulating decarbonization strategies tailored for Hard-to-Abate sectors. Harishankar's expertise also lies in the transformative conversion of biomass, municipal solid waste (MSW), and plastics into biofuels and chemicals, contributing significantly to the circular economy. This platform provides an insightful glimpse into Harishankar's academic journey and offers a detailed exploration of his research interests and accomplishments in these vital fields of study.

Sustainable process design for product/process using LCA approach

Passionate about advancing sustainable process design, my research focuses on integrating Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) methodologies to enhance the environmental performance of products and processes. I am dedicated to exploring innovative solutions that minimize environmental impact throughout the entire lifecycle.

Design of sustainable roadmap for Net-zero targets

My research revolves around crafting sustainable roadmaps tailored for achieving Net-zero targets, employing strategic design principles. I am committed to developing comprehensive frameworks that guide organizations toward environmentally responsible practices and help navigate the path to a Net-zero future.

Develop decarbonization strategies for Hard-to-Abate sectors

Focused on tackling the challenges of Hard-to-Abate sectors, my research centers on devising effective decarbonization strategies. I aim to pioneer innovative approaches that drive emission reductions in industries traditionally resistant to change, contributing to a sustainable and low-carbon future.

Biomass/MSW/Plastics to bio-fuels and chemicals in circular economy

Passionate about advancing circular economy practices, my research expertise lies in the transformative conversion of Biomass, Municipal Solid Waste (MSW), and Plastics into bio-fuels and chemicals. I am dedicated to unraveling sustainable solutions that harness waste streams for renewable energy and resource recovery.
