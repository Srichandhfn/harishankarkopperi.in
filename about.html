Areas of interests

The areas of interest encompass a multifaceted approach to sustainable process design, primarily focusing on the integration of Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) methodologies. The objective is to create environmentally responsible and resource-efficient products or processes. A key aspect involves crafting a sustainable roadmap geared towards achieving Net-zero targets, addressing the urgency of mitigating climate change. Additionally, the exploration of decarbonization strategies specifically for Hard-to-Abate sectors demonstrates a commitment to overcoming challenges in industries with high carbon footprints. Embracing the circular economy concept, the endeavor extends to the conversion of Biomass, Municipal Solid Waste (MSW), and Plastics into bio-fuels and chemicals. This circular approach not only aids in waste reduction but also contributes to the creation of renewable resources, aligning with the broader goals of sustainability and environmental stewardship.
